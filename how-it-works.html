Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if an item is authentic?

RareFind implements a strict verification process for premium items. Look for the "Verified Authentic" badge on listings. Additionally, seller ratings and detailed descriptions can help assess authenticity. For high-value purchases, we offer optional authentication services.

What fees does RareFind charge?

Buyers pay no fee to bid or win auctions. Sellers pay a 5% commission on successful sales, plus a small listing fee for premium placement. We keep our fees competitive to ensure both buyers and sellers get the best value.

How is shipping handled?

Sellers specify shipping options and costs in their listings. After winning an auction, buyers can select their preferred shipping method from those offered by the seller. Our system tracks all shipments and provides updates to both parties.

What if I'm not satisfied with my purchase?

RareFind offers buyer protection on all purchases. If an item significantly differs from its description, our resolution team will help mediate. For items with authentication guarantees, we offer a full refund if proven inauthentic.
